## Saturday, April 22, 2017 

Not a bad year for billionaires. In 2016 they increased their numbers by 13% to 2,043 from 1,810 last year. Their total net worth rose by 18% to $7.67 trillion, also a record. (This is what that number looks like : 7,670,000,000,000,000,000)

Oh and one of them was elected President of the USA.
