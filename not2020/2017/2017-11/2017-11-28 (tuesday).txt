## Tuesday, November 28, 2017> Essentially, the avant garde is about changing the world. It must be, otherwise it wouldn't exist. People tend to think nothing's happening because it's not happening in music. It's happening in quantum physics. If you want to read the new avant garde, read quantum physics.

Penny Rimbaud (of Crass) in [an interview in The Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/music/2017/nov/27/alternative-artists-interviews-thurston-moore-black-madonna)
